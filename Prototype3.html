Daily Briefing

This daily briefing provides a detailed overview of the European Air Traffic situation , based on daily updated data and showing the situation at State, airport, airline and ACC levels.

It also provides views per market segments, aircraft manufacturers, fuel prices, aviation sustainability, punctuality and ATFM delays.

Network status

12 March:

En-route ATFM delays slightly better yesterday than last Saturday but still at 3.83 minutes per flight. Over the last 7 days, France represented 81% of the total en-route ATFM delays.

Arrival punctuality was at 67% with 94% of scheduled flights operated.
